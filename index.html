Our Process

How We
Work

A transparent, structured process from first contact to project close — no surprises, no liability gaps.

01

Scope & Assess

We evaluate your property, document existing conditions, and define the full scope of work before any contractor is engaged.

02

Build Bid Package

We source 2–3 vetted, licensed contractors for your trade, write competitive scope documents, and collect qualified bids on your behalf.

03

You Select & Hire

You review the options, compare pricing, and hire the contractor directly. We ensure all licenses and insurance are current before selection.

04

We Manage & Track

We manage the project on your behalf — schedules, inspections, documentation — with full real-time visibility through Projex.

Why It Matters

The Old Way
vs. The Right Way

Without MEC
  • Hiring contractors without verifying license or insurance
  • Receiving a single bid with no competitive pressure
  • Liability exposure from uninsured or misclassified workers

With MEC

  • Independent owner’s rep managing your project start to finish
  • Detailed scope docs reduce change orders before work begins
